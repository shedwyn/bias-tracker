Welcome to the Incident Tracker Program

The Merriam-Webster on-line dictionary defines "bias" as: an inclination of temperament or outlook; especially : a personal and sometimes unreasoned judgment

"an inclination of temperament or outlook" - we all have it and the only thing that makes that bias negative, neutral, or positive is how we react to or use our biases.

The intention behind this program was to give the user the opportunity to record any incident at work (or school), be it "negative" or "positive", when the details (and emotion) are fresh so that s/he may then review the incident at a later date more objectively. Additionally, s/he may track the tone of interactions with co-workers over time to identify both potential allies as well as those with whom the user needs to change his or her method of communication to better their working relationship.

If you find yourself in regular conflict with a co-worker or fellow student, be objective, consider your own biases, and consider discussing with a supervisor, human resources professional, or fully objective person who can give you additional insight. Be prepared to change the way that YOU communicate in order to improve the team result!

Above all - this program is about (re)evaluation, education and empowerment
